The Rabbit

Rabbits get grumpy if you intrude on their space. Just like some humans ;)

  • Family: Leporidae
  • Habitat: Meadows, woods, forests, grasslands, deserts, wetlands and some households
  • Specification: Lives in groups (colony/nest)
  • Diet: Everything that is green and cannot crawl away

Rabbits are small mammals in the family of Leporidae found in several parts of the world. There are eight different genera in the family classified as rabbits. The male is called a buck and the female is a doe; a young rabbit is a kitten or kit.
